Privacy Policy - HR Recruitment Assistant

Effective Date: June 30, 2025

In short: We respect your privacy. This add-on processes data locally within your Google Sheets and does not transmit personal information to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

The HR Recruitment Assistant add-on processes the following data within your Google Sheets:

  • Candidate names and contact information
  • CV/Resume content and links
  • Recruitment status and notes
  • Interview schedules and feedback

2. How We Use Information

Data is used solely to provide recruitment management functionality:

  • Generating automated reports
  • Tracking candidate progress
  • Sending scheduled notifications
  • Organizing recruitment data

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data remains within your Google Sheets
  • We do not store personal data on external servers
  • Data is subject to Google's security measures
  • You maintain full control over your data
