🆘 Support - HR Recruitment Assistant

🚀 Quick Start Guide

  • Install the add-on from Google Workspace Marketplace
  • Open Google Sheets™ and find "HR Recruitment Assistant" in the Extensions menu
  • Configure your recruitment sheet structure
  • Start tracking candidates and generating reports

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I install the add-on?

A: Go to Google Workspace Marketplace, search for "HR Recruitment Assistant", and click Install.

Q: Can I use this add-on with existing spreadsheets?

A: Yes! The add-on can work with your existing recruitment data in Google Sheets™.

Q: Is my candidate data secure?

A: Yes, all data remains in your Google Sheets™ and is protected by Google's security measures.

🔧 Troubleshooting

  • Add-on not appearing: Refresh the page and check Extensions menu
  • Permission errors: Make sure you have edit access to the Google Sheet
  • Email notifications not working: Check your Gmail settings and permissions
