What is HR Recruitment Assistant?

HR Recruitment Assistant is a comprehensive Google Apps Script application that transforms how organizations manage their recruitment process. Built specifically for Google Workspace users, it integrates seamlessly with Google Sheets, Gmail, and Google Drive to create an intelligent recruitment management system.

This application addresses the common challenges faced by HR professionals: manual candidate tracking, time-consuming CV screening, inconsistent communication, and lack of recruitment analytics. By automating these processes, organizations can focus on what matters most - finding the right talent.
